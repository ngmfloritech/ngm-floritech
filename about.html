Our Story

Founded in 2012, NGM Floritech has grown with one simple belief — that nature’s best comes from pure, organic care. From our first Gerbera bloom to today’s vibrant fields of flowers and vegetables, every harvest reflects our dedication to eco-friendly, chemical-free farming. Over the years, we’ve built more than a farm — we’ve built a promise of freshness, quality, and sustainability that reaches every customer with love and authenticity
