Our Story

Founded in 2012, NGM Floritech has grown with one simple belief — that nature’s best comes from pure, organic care. Although we are relatively new to the industry, our organization has quickly carved out a strong presence in the market by specializing in both retail and wholesale of cut flowers. We are ensuring that only the highest quality flowers reach our customers. Our company takes pride in offering a wide range of innovative, attractive, and visually stunning floral products. In addition, we have the expertise to create customized arrangements tailored to the specific preferences and requirements of our clients. We primarily serve the southern regions of India, including key cities such as Mumbai, Kolkata, Bangalore, Andhra Pradesh, Kerala, and Chennai.
